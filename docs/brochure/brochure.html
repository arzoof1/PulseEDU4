✓  Privacy, Security & Compliance

Vetted, protected, and built for student privacy.

PulseEDU is engineered from the ground up to support FERPA and COPPA compliance. Student data is treated as what it is — protected educational information — with isolation, access controls, and audit trails at the core of the architecture, not bolted on afterward.
§

FERPA

Family Educational Rights & Privacy Act
  • Education records are restricted by strict, role-based access — staff see only what their role permits.
  • Guardian access is limited to their own student(s) through verified, admin-issued portal invites.
  • Sensitive actions are recorded in audit logs, supporting accountability and records requests.
  • Student data is not sold and is used to deliver the school’s educational services.
♥

COPPA

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act
  • Designed for the school-authorized consent model for K–12, acting on the district’s behalf.
  • Configurable consent and visibility controls let districts govern what is shared, in line with their policies.
  • No third-party advertising and no behavioral ad tracking of students.
  • Data collection is focused on what is needed to serve students and run school operations.
Tenant Isolation

Records are school-scoped, keeping each school’s data separated by tenant across core student records.

Secured Access

Role-based permissions and authenticated sessions gate access across the platform.

Protected Storage

Uploaded files are access-controlled with per-school ACLs and tenant-bound permissions.
