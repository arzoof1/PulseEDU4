# Demo Script — School Tours (the full CRM, with accountability)

This is not just a pretty "brag page" — it's a complete **enrollment-lead CRM**:
every family inquiry becomes a tracked lead with an **owner**, a **response
clock**, **stages**, and a **conversion outcome**, so nothing falls through the
cracks and you can prove it.

## 1. The 30-second pitch

A family finds your **public brag page**, requests a tour, and instantly becomes
a **lead** in your pipeline. Someone is **assigned to own it**, a clock starts
ticking, and the lead moves **New → Contacted → Scheduled → Toured → Closed**
with an **outcome** (Enrolled / Deciding / Chose Other). Overdue follow-ups
light up red. At the end you get a **conversion report** — how many inquiries
became enrollments, and who's accountable for each.

## 2. Who to log in as / what to open

- **Family's view (public, no login):** `…/tour/1` — Parrott's bilingual
  (EN/ES) brag page + tour-request form.
- **Staff CRM view:** log in as **Chris Clifford**, open **Settings → School
  Tours** (the lead pipeline + brag-page editor). Requires the **Manage Tours**
  permission.

## 3. The live demo — step-by-step

**Scene 1 — The family experience.** Open `…/tour/1`. Scroll the brag page —
headline, photos, highlights, district branding. Flip **EN ⇄ ES**. *"This is
your public front door; the link goes on your website and in QR codes."* Fill
in the **tour-request form** and submit.

**Scene 2 — A lead is born.** Switch to **Settings → School Tours**. The new
request sits in **New**. *"The moment they hit submit, it's a tracked lead —
and a notification email goes to the team."*

**Scene 3 — Accountability: assign an owner + the clock.** Assign the lead an
**owner**. Point to the **response clock / overdue** indicator. *"Every lead has
a name on it and a timer. If we don't contact them in time, it turns **red** —
no more leads dying in someone's inbox."*

**Scene 4 — Move it through the pipeline.** Advance the lead **Contacted →
Scheduled → Toured**, logging notes on the **activity timeline** as you go.
*"The whole history lives on the lead — every call, every note, every stage
change, with who did it and when."*

**Scene 5 — Close with an outcome.** Mark it **Closed** and pick the
**outcome**: **Enrolled**, **Deciding**, or **Chose Other**. *"This is what
turns activity into a number."*

**Scene 6 — Leave-behinds (PDFs).** Generate the **brag-sheet** (pre-tour) and
**post-tour** PDFs. *"Polished take-homes, on brand, in one click."* (These
**download** to disk — open them from there.)

**Scene 7 — The conversion report.** Show the **conversion report**: inquiries
→ tours → enrollments, broken down by **stage, outcome, and source**, with a
**conversion rate**. *"Now the principal can see exactly how many families we
won, and where we're losing them."*

## 4. Talking points & objection handling

- **Sales:** turns word-of-mouth and walk-ins into a measurable enrollment
  funnel — and enrollment is dollars.
- **Training:** owners + the overdue clock = accountability; the activity
  timeline = an auditable record; outcomes = the conversion number leadership
  asks for.
- *"We already use email."* Email loses the thread, the owner, and the report.
  This keeps all three in one place.

## 5. Notes & gotchas

- Public links/QRs resolve to the **published** domain (not the dev preview), so
  test them from the live site.
- Pipeline stages are fixed: **new → contacted → scheduled → toured → closed**;
  outcomes are **enrolled / deciding / chose_other**.

## 6. Quick reference

- Public brag page: `…/tour/1` (bilingual EN/ES, tour-request form)
- Staff CRM: **Settings → School Tours** (pipeline, owners, overdue clock,
  activity timeline, conversion report, brag-page editor)
- PDFs: brag-sheet + post-tour leave-behinds (download to disk)
- Permission: **Manage Tours**
