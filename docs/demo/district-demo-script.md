# PulseEDU District Demo — Presenter Script

**Demo school:** D.S. Parrott Middle School (school_id 1)
**Deck:** "PulseEDU District Demo" slides artifact (16 slides)
**Through-line:** This is not a feature tour. It is one school day, shown end to
end, across every audience — students, teachers, families, and administrators.

Each section below maps to a deck slide and pairs the talking points with the
live action to take in the app. Screens marked **LIVE** are login-gated and must
be demonstrated in the running app (see `district-demo-shot-list.md`). Screens
marked **PUBLIC** are already embedded as screenshots in the deck.

---

## Slide 1 — Title: "The operating system for your schools"

Open on the promise, not the product:

> "Today you won't see eighteen disconnected tools. You'll see one platform —
> the same data following a student from the hallway, to the classroom, to the
> family's inbox, to your leadership dashboard. Everything is real data from
> D.S. Parrott Middle."

Set the expectation: eight stops, one school day.

---

## Slide 2 — The demonstration flow

Walk the eight stops in ten seconds each. Plant the key idea: **they connect.**
"The same badge scan that opens a hall pass also takes attendance and awards a
behavior point. You'll see that in the first two minutes."

---

## Section 1 — Student Experience

### Slide 3 — Hall Pass & Kiosk (LIVE: kiosk + teacher queue)
- "A student needs to leave the room. No paper, no interrupting the teacher."
- **LIVE:** At the kiosk in Amy Brown's room, scan a student badge, choose a
  destination.
- Show a destination **at capacity** — the student joins a period-aware waiting
  line that resets on the bell.
- Switch to the teacher's **Companion Queue** and push a waiting student through
  with **Go Now**.
- Show the **live timer**, then the **return check-in**.
- Land it: "One action. The hallway is now accounted for."

### Slide 4 — Attendance, PBIS & Tour (PUBLIC: tour page)
- "Here's the part most schools miss: that same scan did three things."
- Attendance was marked. A PBIS point posted automatically. The pass is logged.
- **PUBLIC:** Open the school tour page — "this is how a prospective family
  first meets D.S. Parrott, in English or Spanish, before they ever walk in."

---

## Section 2 — Teacher Experience

### Slide 5 — Teacher Roster (LIVE: Amy Brown's roster)
- "We just made the student's day easier. Now the teacher's."
- **LIVE:** Open **Amy Brown's Grade 7 Math** roster. Point at FAST benchmark
  color-coding, the bottom standards, and the ESE/504/ELL + safety flags.
- "This replaces a spreadsheet she used to keep by hand. And the Core Team sees
  the exact same roster — one source of truth."

### Slide 6 — Small Groups & Accommodations (LIVE: Pamela Martin)
- **LIVE:** Group students by a shared weak standard, form a small group, and
  log a support session. Show the history building over time.
- **LIVE:** Open **Pamela Martin's** accommodations for ~5 students in ELA.
- "Compliance stops being a binder. It's in the daily workflow."

## Section 3 — Student Support (the emotional center)

### Slide 7 — HeartBEAT, the Invisible Student (LIVE: Noah Xu)
- Slow down. Say the line out loud:

> **"Every student should be known by someone."**

- "Some students aren't failing and aren't in trouble — so no one's watching.
  They can go a whole year without one meaningful adult connection."
- **LIVE:** Open **Noah Xu** — show the invisible-student flag and the
  connection history that explains *why* the system surfaced him.
- "PulseEDU finds these students before the year slips by."

## Section 4 — MTSS

### Slide 8 — Tiered Interventions (LIVE: Tier 3 academic plans)
- "Intervention is only real if it's documented. Watch two months of it."
- **LIVE:** Open a Tier 3 academic plan — **Alina Maddox (ELA)**, **Amelia
  Abbott (math)**, **Sienna Osborne (math)**. Show the timeline: multiple

## Section 5 — AST & Comp Time

### Slide 9 — Earned time, fully accounted (LIVE: approval workflow)
- "Staff time is a real budget line and a real grievance risk. Here's the audit
  trail."
- **LIVE:** As **Demo Admin**, walk the five steps: submit → approve → complete
  → verify → bank. Show **EST Comp Time** banking for **Teresa Holloway**.
- "Both AST and Comp Time, end to end, defensible at payroll."

---

## Section 6 — Family Communication + PulseDNA AI (the wow moment)

### Slide 10 — PulseDNA AI (LIVE: generate from a rough idea)
- "Every principal knows this problem: you have thirty seconds and a great
  moment to share, but no time to write it three ways."
- **LIVE:** Type the rough idea exactly:
  > "Volleyball team won tonight. Great crowd. Proud of our kids."
- Generate all three: **family message**, **social post**, **teleprompter
  script** — in the school's saved voice.
- Say the line:

> **"AI should not replace the principal's voice. It should learn the school's
> voice."**

- If you have a before/after, show the generic AI version next to the
  DNA-trained version.

### Slide 11 — Family HeartBEAT / Parent Portal (LIVE: a parent's own child)
- "If PulseDNA is the school's voice going out, this is the family's window
  coming in — the same HeartBEAT staff see, shared directly with the family."
- **LIVE:** Open the Parent Portal as a parent and walk one child's day:
  hall passes and attendance, **tardies and lost instructional minutes**
  (kept honest), **FAST scores with the next steps** that follow, and the
  **interventions and accommodations** in place.
- Show an **important school message acknowledged in one tap**.
- Note **sibling switching**, configurable section visibility, and **PDF
  export** — one secure, per-student record for the family.

## Section 7 — Leadership Intelligence

### Slide 12 — Insights Hub (LIVE: Algebra readiness → student drill-down)
- Say the line:

> **"Data should not simply tell us what happened. It should guide what we do
> next."**

- **LIVE:** Open Algebra performance / readiness. Then **drill from a chart
  into a single student profile** to prove it's not just a dashboard.

### Slide 13 — FAST Coverage Report (LIVE: gaps by standard)
- "Scores tell you how students did. Coverage tells you whether they were even
  taught what the test measures."
- **LIVE:** Show standards analyzed, % curriculum taught, and instructional gaps
  for Grade 7 — surfaced early enough to act on.

## Section 8 — School Operations

### Slide 14 — Event Ticketing (LIVE: create + double-scan)
- **LIVE:** Create an event with a per-grade quota. Show a QR ticket. Then
  **scan the same ticket twice** to show the first-scan-wins duplicate block.
- "No cash box, no clipboard, no double entry."

### Slide 15 — Digital Signage (PUBLIC: live House Cup)
- **PUBLIC:** Show the live PBIS House Cup signage (embedded). Mention the
  remote that drives every TV on a playlist without re-entering URLs.
- "School culture, made visible on every screen."

## Slide 16 — The thesis (close)

Land it verbatim:

> "PulseEDU is not another software product. It is the operating system that
> connects students, teachers, families, and administrators into a single,
> actionable platform."

Tie back: "One platform. Every adult in the building. One school day — the one
you just watched."
