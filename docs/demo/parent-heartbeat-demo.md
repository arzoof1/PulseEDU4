# Demo Script — HeartBEAT to Families (every week)

The payoff of everything else: families get a **secure portal** and an
**automatic weekly email** summarizing their child — PBIS, hall passes,
attendance, accommodations, and staff notes — with **zero extra work** for
staff. This is the "we partner with parents" story.

## 1. The 30-second pitch

Each week (Fridays by default), every opted-in family gets a **Weekly
HeartBEAT** email — a clean PDF snapshot of their child's week: points earned,
on-time streak, hall passes,
tardies, accommodations, and supportive notes from staff. They can also log into
the **Parent Portal** anytime, switch between siblings, and download the same
snapshot. *"One email a week keeps families in the loop without a single phone
call."*

## 2. Who to log in as / what to open

- **Admin view:** log in as **Chris Clifford** → **Admin Hub → Parent Access**
  (invites + "Preview as parent"). Section visibility lives in **Settings →
  Parent Portal Sections**.
- **Family view:** the portal at `…/parent`. For the demo, use **Preview as
  parent** (no real parent login needed).

## 3. The live demo — step-by-step

**Scene 1 — Inviting a family.** In **Parent Access**, show **Invite parent**
(sends a magic-link email via the email integration) and **Send to all
eligible** for bulk onboarding. *"Parents set their own password from the link —
no accounts for us to manage."*

**Scene 2 — The family's portal (Preview as parent).** Click **Preview as
parent** to open a student's HeartBEAT. Walk the sections: **PBIS points** (total
/ this week), **Hall passes**, **Attendance** (tardies + on-time streak),
**Accommodations**, **Staff notes/supports**, **House**, and event **Tickets**.
*"Everything we demoed today, in plain language for a parent."*

**Scene 3 — Siblings + privacy controls.** If multiple kids are linked, show the
**sibling switcher**. Then show **Settings → Parent Portal Sections** —
*"the school decides which sections families can see; sensitive items can be
hidden district-wide."*

**Scene 4 — The weekly email (the headline).** Explain the **Weekly HeartBEAT
email**: opted-in parents at schools that allow it get the **same snapshot as a
PDF attachment**, sent automatically each week. Because it's a scheduled send,
**don't wait for it live** — instead click **Download PDF** in the portal to show
the **same snapshot/template PDF** families receive. *"What you're downloading is
the same snapshot that lands in their inbox each week — automatically."*

**Scene 5 — Opt-in / cadence.** Note that it's **opt-in** (parent preference)
**and** school-allowed, and in normal operation it's **deduped** (a family isn't
re-mailed within the same week). *"Families choose in; the school stays in
control."*

## 4. Talking points & objection handling

- **Sales:** family engagement is a board-level metric — this delivers it on
  autopilot, in the family's hands, every week.
- **Training:** the weekly email reuses the **same snapshot** the parent sees on
  the dashboard, so the email and the portal always match.
- *"Will this flood parents?"* No — one weekly digest, opt-in, never duplicated;
  sensitive sections are hidden by school policy.
- **IDs:** the portal shows the **local SIS ID** only — never the state FLEID.

## 5. Quick reference

- Family portal: `…/parent`
- Admin: **Admin Hub → Parent Access** (Invite parent · Send to all eligible ·
  **Preview as parent**)
- Visibility: **Settings → Parent Portal Sections**
- Snapshot: **Download PDF** in the portal = the same snapshot/template as the
  weekly email attachment
- Weekly email: **opt-in** + school-allowed, automatic, Fridays by default
  (configurable), deduped per week in normal operation
