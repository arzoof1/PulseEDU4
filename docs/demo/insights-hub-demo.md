# Demo Script — Insights Hub

Six leadership dashboards over the same live data your teachers create —
**Engagement, Behavior, Academics, SEB/SEL, Equity, Early Warning** — with
filters and one-click **drill-down to a single student**.

## 1. The 30-second pitch

Everything happening in PulseEDU rolls up into **Insights**: trends, top-N
lists, and demographic breakdowns across six dashboards. Filter by grade, PM
window, or subgroup — then click any student and land in their full profile.
*"From a district-level number to one child in two clicks."*

## 2. Who to log in as / what to open

- Drive as **Chris Clifford** (SuperUser / Core Team) — full access; he can also
  **switch schools** to show district vs. building. Open **Insights** from the nav.
- *(Plain teachers see a version scoped to their own roster — worth mentioning.)*

## 3. The live demo — step-by-step

**Scene 1 — The hub.** Open **Insights**. Walk the six tabs so the audience sees
the breadth: **Engagement · Behavior · Academics · SEB/SEL · Equity · Early
Warning.**

**Scene 2 — Filters that matter.** On a dashboard, apply the shared filters:
**grade** chips, **PM window**, subject **ELA/Math**, and subgroup toggles
(**ESE, 504, Tier 2+, Tier 3**). *"The same screen answers a dozen different
questions."*

**Scene 3 — Academics.** Show the **academic trajectory** (PM1 → PM2 → PM3) and
learning-gain view. *"This is the growth story, school-wide — and it ties to the
green-check teachers see on their rosters."*

**Scene 4 — Engagement & Behavior.** Engagement: **tardy trends** and on-time
streaks (the same on-time data from the PBIS track, rolled up). Behavior:
incident aggregates and top-N by reason/location. *"Attendance and behavior,
trended — not a once-a-year report."*

**Scene 5 — Equity.** Show the **equity gap** view: disproportionate
representation across subgroups (ESE / 504 / race-ethnicity). *"This is the hard
question leadership has to answer — here it's in front of you, not buried."*

**Scene 6 — Early Warning → one student.** Open **Early Warning** (A-B-C risk:
Attendance, Behavior, Course/Academics). Open a **top-N risk** list and **click a
student** → their **profile**. *"That's the move: a worrying trend becomes a
named kid you can actually help today."*

## 4. Talking points & objection handling

- **Sales:** turns daily operational data into leadership insight with **no extra
  data entry** — it's already flowing in from Hall Pass, PBIS, attendance, FAST.
- **Training:** Insights, the Teacher Roster, and the PBIS Hub share the **same
  underlying logic**, so a number in Insights matches the roster flag and the
  Needs-Attention list.
- *"Is this another dashboard tool?"* It's fed by the **same** product staff use
  all day, so it's always current and consistent — not a separate import.

## 5. Quick reference

- Open **Insights** (nav). Role gate: admin / Core Team (teachers see own roster).
- Tabs: **Engagement, Behavior, Academics, SEB/SEL, Equity, Early Warning**
- Filters: grade, PM window, ELA/Math, ESE/504/Tier 2+/Tier 3
- **Click any student name → student profile** (the drill-down)
