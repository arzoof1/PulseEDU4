## What this is

**PBIS Points** is how you (and every adult in the building)
recognize students for positive behavior. Points feed three things:
- **House standings** (the live scoreboard on TVs)
- **Spotlight rewards** (random featured students at assemblies)
- **The PBIS Store** (students cash points in for items)

## Three ways to award points

1. **From the PBIS Hub** — open the page, search a student or pick
   from "Recent students", choose a reason (e.g. "Helping a peer"),
   set the point amount. Submit.
2. **From Teacher Roster** — every row has a **+ PBIS** quick button.
   Faster for in-the-moment recognition without leaving your roster.
3. **Bulk award** — Hub → **Bulk award** → pick a section or houses
   → reason → submit. Good for "whole class earned 5 points for…"

## Setting amounts

- Each reason has a **default** value set by your admin (typical
  range 1–5).
- You can override per award if your role allows it (varies by school
  setting).

## Including a quick note

The **Add note** field is optional but high-value — it gives the
student (and their parent in the portal) context: "Caught helping
the new kid find his class." Notes show on the student's profile
timeline.

## What students see

- **Their house's standing** on hallway TVs.
- **Their personal point balance** at the PBIS Store kiosk or in
  the parent portal (if enabled).
- **Spotlight reveals** — randomly featured at assemblies.

## House standings & the Spotlight governor

Every point you award goes to the student's house. PulseEDU has a
**runaway-leader guard**: if one house pulls more than ~1500 points
ahead, the Spotlight reward pool quietly tiers so the trailing
houses still have a real shot at being featured. You don't need to
do anything — it just keeps the race competitive.

## Negative points / corrections

Some schools enable **PBIS negative entries** (for redirections /
minor infractions). If your school has them on, the Hub has a
**Redirect** tab. Negative points feed Insights & MTSS — they don't
deduct from house standings unless your school configured it that
way.

## Common uses

- **Daily**: 3–5 quick recognitions per period from the roster.
- **Friday**: bulk award for the class that hit a weekly goal.
- **Assembly**: Spotlight pick (PBIS Hub → Spotlight) reveals 1–10
  points to a featured student per house.

## Tips

- **Reasons matter more than amounts** — kids and parents read the
  reason in the timeline. Specific > generic.
- If a student's running total looks wrong, click their name in the
  Hub → **History** tab shows every entry with the awarder and reason.
