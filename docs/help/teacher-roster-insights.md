## What this is

The **Insights** tab on Teacher Roster surfaces **patterns the eye
misses** — students trending the wrong way on behavior, hall passes
spiking, attendance drifting, FAST scores sliding between PM windows,
or learning gains stalling.

It's your weekly "who needs me?" screen.

## Sections you'll see

1. **Movers & shakers** — biggest PM-to-PM jumps, up and down. Catch
   the kid who quietly dropped a level before the parent emails.
2. **Behavior watch** — students with rising PBIS-negative entries,
   referrals, or pass overuse in the last 14 days.
3. **Attendance flags** — tardies clustering, absences clustering.
4. **Learning Gains scoreboard** — class % showing gains, broken
   down by subject + level band.
5. **Suggested actions** — one-click "send a positive note home",
   "open MTSS Tier 2 plan", "start a behavior support note" tied to
   specific students.

## Reading a card

Each insight card has:
- **What's happening** — plain English ("4 students moved from L3 →
  L2 in Math between PM1 and PM2").
- **Who** — names listed, click to open profile.
- **Why it might matter** — short context.
- **Action buttons** — direct shortcuts to the right tool.

## Filters

- **Time window**: 7 / 14 / 30 days.
- **Subject**: filters PM and benchmark cards.

## Tip

Insights regenerates whenever a PM upload or new behavior data lands.
Check it Monday mornings — that's when most schools update data over
the weekend.
