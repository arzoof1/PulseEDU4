## What this is

The **PulseDNA Studio** helps Core Team write and record warm,
on-brand video messages to families. It has two parts:

1. **PulseDNA profile** — your school's saved "voice" (tone, values,
   sign-off). The AI uses it as background context when it drafts a
   message so everything sounds like your school.
2. **Recording studio** — a built-in camera + **teleprompter** so you
   can read your message on camera without memorizing it.

You reach it from the **Family** group in the sidebar (Core Team only).

## Set up your PulseDNA profile (once)

1. Open **PulseDNA Studio** and go to the **Profile** tab.
2. **Upload** an existing profile document (.txt, .md, .pdf, or .docx)
   or **paste** your text into the box.
3. Click **Save profile**.
4. Use the **enable / disable** toggle to control whether the AI uses
   it. You can edit or replace it anytime.

## Draft a message with AI

1. Go to the **Create** tab.
2. Type what you want to say (a few notes is enough), or use **voice
   dictation** to speak it.
3. Click **Draft** — the AI writes a family-ready message in your
   school's voice.
4. Edit the draft until it's right.

## Record a video

1. From the Create tab, click **Record a video** (or **Record a video
   with this script** to load your draft into the teleprompter).
2. Allow the browser to use your **camera** and **microphone** the
   first time.
3. Click **● Record** to start. The timer counts up to the 5-minute
   limit; click **◼ Stop** when done.
4. Review your take, then **Use this take** to keep it or **Record
   again** to retry.

## Use the teleprompter

- **Edit script** — paste or type the words you'll read.
- **Play script** / **Pause script** — start and stop the scrolling.
- **Restart script** — jump back to the top.
- **Speed** — how fast it scrolls; nudge it a little at a time.
- **Text size** and **Line spacing** — make it comfortable to read.
- **Reading width** — narrows the words into a center column so your
  eyes stay near the camera lens.
- **Visible lines** — limits how many lines show at once; set it low
  (2–3) so you read across, not down the page.
- **Condense blank lines** — closes the gaps between paragraphs.

Shortcuts: **Space** plays/pauses, **↑ / ↓** change speed, and **R**
starts/stops recording. A Bluetooth presentation clicker sends these
same keys, so you can drive the studio from across the room.

In the studio itself, click **? Help** (top right) anytime for these
directions.

## Tips for a good take

- Look into the **camera lens**, not at the words.
- Do one practice run with **Play script** before recording.
- Keep the teleprompter window small and near the top so your eyes
  stay close to the lens.
