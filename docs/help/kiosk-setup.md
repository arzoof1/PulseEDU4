## One-time enrollment (per device)

1. On your **teacher computer**, open the **Kiosk** page from the
   sidebar.
2. Click **Enroll a new kiosk**. A 6-digit enrollment code appears
   (good for ~15 minutes).
3. On the **kiosk device** (the iPad / Chromebook in your room), open
   the app URL, go to the kiosk login screen, and type the code.
4. The device is now bound to your room. It will stay enrolled until
   you remove it.

## Daily use

- The kiosk shows your roster (or current period roster if a bell
  schedule is configured) and the destination list.
- Students tap their name → destination → done. Teachers don't need to
  do anything during normal flow.

## Common issues

- **"Code expired"**: codes are good for 15 minutes. Click **Enroll a
  new kiosk** again for a fresh code.
- **Kiosk shows wrong roster**: it follows the schedule for the teacher
  who enrolled it. If you swap rooms, re-enroll on the new device.
- **Kiosk asleep / black screen**: tap to wake. If still black, refresh
  the browser tab.
- **Need to retire a device**: on your teacher computer's Kiosk page,
  find the device in the list and click **Remove**.

## Settings you can change

On the Kiosk page, the **Settings** panel lets you toggle:
- Show student photos (vs. initials only)
- Show a return countdown
- Require a return-reason tap
