Pulse logo generator

Type a brand name, tweak the colors, and download the SVG or PNG. The italic accent word is the part that picks up the bolder weight at the end (e.g. PulseEDU, PulseTV, PulseKinetics).

How sizing works

The viewBox is recalculated to fit your text — the EKG bump always anchors to the right of the wordmark with the same shape as the original PulseEDU mark. Background stays transparent so you can drop the SVG/PNG onto any color.
