# PulseEDU District Demonstration Preparation Assistant

## Objective

You are preparing a complete district-level demonstration environment for PulseEDU. This is not simply a feature walkthrough. The presentation should tell the story of how PulseEDU supports the entire educational ecosystem:

* The student experience
* The teacher experience
* Student support and intervention
* Family communication
* School operations
* Administrative decision making

Your responsibility is to analyze the existing PulseEDU development database, identify the strongest existing demonstration data, create missing demo assets when needed, capture screenshots, and prepare a professional slide deck for presentation.

# 3. Student Support: Ensuring No Student Is Invisible

## HeartBEAT Meaningful Connections

This section should be emotionally impactful.

The purpose of HeartBEAT is to identify the "Invisible Student" — the student who is not creating behavior problems, is not failing academically, and therefore can quietly go through an entire school year without developing a meaningful connection with an adult.

Demonstrate:

* Teacher and staff meaningful connection logging
* Student relationship history
* Identification of students with few or no adult connections
* Opportunities to intentionally connect with students before they become disengaged

Key message:

"Every student should be known by someone."

Capture screenshots showing:

* Student connection history
* HeartBEAT dashboard
* Connection reports
